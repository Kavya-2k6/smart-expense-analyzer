## What problem does this solve?
 
It's the 3rd of the month. There's a BOGO offer at dominos
and you say yes. Then on the 20th your friends want to watch
Spider-Man and now you're broke.
You contributed ₹100 to a birthday gift when you promised ₹300.

Next month you decide to track you expenses but the loop repeats.
you miss a box cricket because of improper money management
 
This app helps you see that connection before it happens.
Not after.
 
## Who is this for?

A college student living on a monthly allowance or part-time
income who keeps running out of money before the month ends.
 
## What can a user do with this?
- Log daily expenses by category
- See a visual breakdown of where money went this month
- Get a simple end-of-month forecast based on current spending
